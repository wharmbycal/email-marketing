AI will probably change small towns like Leighton Buzzard in quieter ways than people expect.

For many local businesses, it won’t replace jobs — it will simply save time. Writing replies, updating websites, handling routine admin. The kind of work that often gets pushed to late evenings.

Used well, that could mean quicker responses and smoother service for customers.

But in a town built on relationships and trust, the human side of business will still matter most.
